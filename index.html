Who am I?

Hi! I'm Emeric Boyadjian, I'm 20 years old and want to become a web and mobile project manager.

My career path

After having obtained a university degree in Multimedia and Internet Technology (MMI) in 2021, I chose to continue my studies in bachelor web development at Epitech Digital. Currently in my second year, I'm progressing with the help of all kinds of projects accompanied by professionals in web development but also in coaching and project management.

My hobbies

Very curious by nature, I have several passions like manga and webtoons. But also basketball, skiing and more recently freestyle skating. I also play guitar, for about 2 years, which I learn by myself.
